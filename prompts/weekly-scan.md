# Weekly Intelligence Scan Prompt

Use this prompt to identify the most significant developments from the past week.

## Prompt

```
What are the most significant security developments from the past 7 days?

Review our knowledge base for:
- New CISA KEV entries
- Emerging threat actor activity
- Notable vulnerabilities or exploits
- Government agency advisories (CISA, UK NCSC, etc.)
- Industry reports or research

For each finding:
1. What happened (1-2 sentences)
2. Why it matters (business impact)
3. Who should care (industries/roles)
4. Recommended action

Prioritize by: Active exploitation > Widespread impact > Novel technique
```

## Variations

### Quick Scan (3 items)
```
What are the TOP 3 security developments this week that executives need to know?
One sentence each, focus on business impact.
```

### Deep Scan (comprehensive)
```
Generate a comprehensive weekly intelligence brief covering:
1. Critical vulnerabilities (CISA KEV additions)
2. Threat actor activity
3. Emerging attack techniques
4. Regulatory/compliance updates
5. One trend to watch

Include MITRE ATT&CK mappings where applicable.
```
